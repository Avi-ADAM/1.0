Frequently asked questions

What exactly is 1💗1?

1💗1 is a platform for managing agreements and collaborations. It enables people to create groups ('rikmas') around shared goals, negotiate and reach agreements in a transparent and organized way.

What's the difference between 1💗1 and the agreement site?

1💗1 (1lev1.com) is the collaboration platform – the place where you create rikmas, manage projects and work together. agreement.1lev1.com is an independent site dedicated to the global vision of the worldwide agreement.

Is it mandatory to visit the full agreement site?

No! You can agree to the basic agreement (checkbox in the form) and continue straight to registration. The full agreement site is an optional extension for those who want to join the global vision.

What does 'rikma' mean?

A rikma is a group in 1💗1 created around a shared goal. The metaphor is a biological colony: cells that cooperate and together create something greater, while each preserves its own cellular autonomy. Each member is a cell, and together they are the rikma.

What happens after registration?

After registration you can join existing rikmas, create a new rikma, manage projects and work with people from around the world – all by mutual consent.
